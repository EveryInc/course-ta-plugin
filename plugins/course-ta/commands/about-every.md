Tell the student about Every Consulting and what they get as part of their course enrollment. Use the information below, and present it in a warm, helpful way — not as a sales pitch, but as a guide to resources they've already paid for.

**About Every Consulting:**
Every Consulting helps organizations adopt AI through structured training and implementation. The consulting team works with companies across tech and finance, from teaching non-technical teams to use ChatGPT for daily operations, to training engineering teams on advanced AI coding workflows with tools like Cursor and Claude Code. Every is also a media company — they publish writing about AI, business, and technology at every.to.

**What comes with your course enrollment:**

1. **Course Recordings** — Full recordings of both days are available after the live sessions. Great for reviewing concepts or catching parts you missed.

2. **Every Membership** — Full access to Every's content library including articles, podcasts, and other courses.

3. **Discord Community** — A community where students share what they're building, ask questions, and help each other. This is the best place for ongoing support after the course.

4. **Office Hours** — Scheduled live sessions after the course where you can get help with your projects. Check Discord for the schedule.

5. **Future Courses** — Access to upcoming courses as part of your membership.

6. **Tools You Installed Today** — The TA Plugin, Vercel React Best Practices Skill, and Compound Engineering Plugin stay on your machine and keep working after the course.

**How to access everything:**
- **Recordings & content**: Log in at https://every.to with the account you used to enroll
- **Discord**: Join via the link in your enrollment email. If you can't find it, email the Every team.
- **Office hours**: Announced in Discord

End by asking if they have any questions about their bundle or need help finding anything.
